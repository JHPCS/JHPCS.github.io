Welcome to the Random Fact Generator Info Page!

About This Page

This page was created with the intention of providing valuable information and services to its users.
The main goal is to educate the users with random facts and events in history.
These facts you may or may not ever need to know, the only way to find out is by clicking that red random fact button!

Functions

  • "Press for a random fact!": This button is the main part of the website, it generates a random fact to display!.
  • "Undo Last Fact": This button displays the fact that was previously displayed.
    You may need this button for a couple reasons, want to show your friends some of the previous facts?
    Well you sure can with this button.
    Maybe you clicked too fast and missed the last one, you can go back and read the one you missed!
  • "Read Fact Aloud": Perfect if you have visual issues or maybe you just want to not read.
    Well this button says the fact instead of making you read.
  • "Stop Reading": This stops the fact from being read aloud.
  • The hamburger menu at the top left of the screen: If you click this menu 4 hyperlinks appear.
  • "Contact": First option in the menu.
    This allows you to ask questions, or correct one of the facts or functions on my website.
  • "Suggestions": Second option in the menu.
    This allows you to suggest a idea to me, this can be another function or a fact.
  • "info": Third option in the menu and where you are right now!
    This displays info about the website
  • "Help-desk": Final option in the menu.
    See frequently asked questions.
