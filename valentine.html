Welcome to the Valentine's Riddle!

Can you solve this Riddle? I'm cold and square, a mystery lies within, Open me up, and your treasures begin. In the depths, I hold both feast and fare, What am I? A box of cool air.

Dressed in silk or cotton fine, With seams and stitches all in line. Yet, no heart beats beneath the dress, Nor thoughts to ponder or to express.
